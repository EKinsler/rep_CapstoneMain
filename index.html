JC the Caster

JC can switch from a casual gamer to a YouTuber version of Bob Ross. His main desire on the platform is to spread positivity, and as stated on his twitter account "to capture the connections the internet forgets."

RaccoonEggs

In early 2018 and 2019, RaccoonEggs sprung into YouTube popularity due to his snarky wit and distinct sense of humor while playing the popular FPS from VALVE games: 'Counter Strike: Global Offensive' (CS:GO). Even though, he is highly inactive now, the legacy he has left for YouTube's gaming community, and the CS:GO will never be forgotten.

SwaggerSouls

Being part of one of the most popular YouTube groups still active today: The MISFITS, faceless Youtube video-gaming channel SwaggerSouls' tone of voice and his interactions with people he meets online and the MISFITS is what he uses to boost his profile and really push himself far in the YouTube algorithm.
