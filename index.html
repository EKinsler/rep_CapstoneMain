A NEW GENERATION OF CONTENT

Based off the video-game 'Undertale' by Toby Fox and community revolving around it, 'THE MULTIVERSE' acts as one of the crowning achievements of the community. Featuring 10 Alternate Universes (AU's) created by fellow members, you follow Frisk, the savior of the race of Monsters going on another journey that is similar and different at the same time. With more quirky and random characters, deep stories and along with hilarious and heart-touching moments, this is an experience for anyone who has had 'Undertale,' speak to them in any way that inspired them to push forward in life and stay determined.
